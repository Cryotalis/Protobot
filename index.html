Features


Search for Shards and Mods

Get all the details and information on any Shard or Mod.
Includes drop locations (Chaos Tier), gilded values (Shards only), upgrade levels (Shards only), applicable Heroes, and gear slot.

Search for Defense Builds

Find various recommended builds by top players for any defense in the game.

Search for Recommended Prices for Items

Get the recommended prices for items.
Prices are sourced from a spreadsheet maintained by market experts and top players.

Calculate your Minimum Ascension and Talent Caps

Calculates the Minimum Ascension and Talent Caps you would have if you Reset on a given Floor with a given amount of Ascension.
Protobot will assess your Minimum Ascension and advise you on whether or not you are ready to Reset.

Check the Drakenfrost Keep Rotation

Find out what weapons and mods are in rotation this week in Drakenfrost Keep.
Also shows which mod is available next rotation, and how much time remains until the next rotation arrives.
