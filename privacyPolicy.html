Protobot

Discord Bot for Dungeon Defenders 2. Made by Cryo#4704.

Privacy and Data Usage Policy

I do not store or collect any user data other than what is necessary. Protobot's database is a public Google Spreadsheet. It is viewable by anyone.

Your ID and Tag are stored whenever you make a post in one of the looking-for-trade channels in the official Dungeon Defenders Discord server, this information is used by the custom slowmode/automod feature of the looking-for-trade channels.

Your Server's ID and Name are stored when you invite Protobot to your server. This is so that settings pertaining to the custom prefix and disable commands features can be stored and tied to your Server.

None of this data is at all sensitive, but if you would like to delete your data for any reason, please contact me (click the Contact button up top). Usual response time is 1 Day.
