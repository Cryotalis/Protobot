About Protobot

Protobot is the first Discord bot I created, and this was my first real step into the world of programming. I taught myself NodeJS and started working on this project in 2019. At the time, the popular DD2Tools website had just been retired along with the DD2Tools Discord bot. The developer behind these tools, Czokalapik, cited a lack of funding and interest in the game. I wanted to create a replacement for the DD2Tools Discord bot, so I started this project. Mr. Juicebags was kind enough to allow the bot into his Discord server when I first started development so I could receive valuable feedback from his community.


I was quickly able to recreate DD2Tools' most prominent feature â€” the shard search command. I then introduced several new functionalities such as mod search, minimum ascension calculation, and drakenfrost rotation status. Shortly afterwards, I convinced the Admin of the official Dungeon Defenders Discord server at the time, Kaoe, to add Protobot to the server. Protobot was kept exclusive to the DD Discord server for a number of years due to Kaoe's influence, but I've since made Protobot available to everyone.


Today, Protobot is in use in over 500 Discord servers. It is open source and uses a publicly available Google spreadsheet as its primary database. I am now the sole Administrator of the official Dungeon Defenders Discord server, and Protobot serves a few special functions in the DD server. An exclusive blue Protobot pet has also since been added to DD2. I gave them out to those who made major contributions to Protobot and/or the community.
